ABOUT LAUFEY

Singer/songwriter and multi-instrumentalist Laufey
brings a jazz-influenced, traditional pop style into the
21st century with soothing songs inspired by artists
such as Ella Fitzgerald, Billie Holiday, Peggy Lee,
Chet Baker, and George Gershwin.
